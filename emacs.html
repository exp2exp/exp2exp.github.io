Emacs and Org Mode

Some of us have been looking at Emacs and Org Mode, along with other tools for researchers and knowledge workers. We gave a short talk at EmacsConf about a preliminary design for an Emacs Hyper Notebook. One of our reasons for starting that project was a sense of dissatisfaction with what could be achievede with Jupyter; see for example: emacs-jupyter remote debugging.

Some of our subsequent discussions about Emacs and Research appear inEmacs Research Group meeting notes.

What have we here?

This page attempts to create a summary of the themes and ideas we’ve been surfacing. It draws on our experiments with Causal Layered Analysis (CLA 16 January 2021, CLA 20 February 2021, CLA 12 April 2021).
