21 April 2021 — Resonances

2021-04-21 — Joe Corneli

Here I describe the resonances that I am thinking about (across the wiki), and compare my writing with the musical genre of ‘sludge’. Then I start to reflect on how people don’t necessarily have incentives to stop and think about how things work. I compare reasonable and unreasonable thinking. Then I talk about how when I was first trying to think about these issues I thought in terms of ‘hypertext’. I’m alluding to Deleuze’s Logic of Sense as far as I can remember what he says there.
