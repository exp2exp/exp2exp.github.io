Study with crowdsourced exercises

“Crowdsourced reading comprehension questions and simple exercises derived from existing Q&A would provide a further route to evaluation.”

So the idea here would be to pay people to come up with some SQuAD-like questions and answers based on existing Stack Exchange problems. This would help build a catalogue of the kinds of things we’re looking for inside of the Q&A items.
