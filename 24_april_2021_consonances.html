24 April 2021 — Consonances

2021-04-24 — Joe Corneli

I’m looking at pages like the Hyperreal Enterprises: Roadmap and Top to reorient myself to what we said when we first created this wiki. I’m thinking about how what we’re doing may be actively opposed in some cases, not simply difficult. My sense is that some of our historical difficulties may have come from the less-developed state of our communication patterns. This got me thinking then about the challenge of employing multiple different disciplinary-styles-of-thinking in a coherent way. I tried to imagine what that thinking might do for us. I considered that ‘known’ approaches are often ‘enough’ which shows us some of our own built-in resistance to finding new methods. Thinking about the difference between participants in a collective endeavour and that collective endeavour itself is then suggested as a sort of philosophical can-opener.
